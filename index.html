Who are we?

Essential Therapeutics is led by Dr Debbie Godden (MBChB FRCEM) who has been an NHS doctor for more than 25 years. I completed my Aromatherapy training at the only NHS-based complementary therapy school — the Natural Health School — and am approved to provide treatments to NHS referred patients. In 2022 I undertook training in Myofascial Release to enable me to offer an extended range of services.

Listed on the FHT Complementary Healthcare Therapist Register
"Essential Therapeutics evolved because I believe complementary therapies have the potential to help many people."

Our services

Myofascial Release

A slow, safe, gentle, effective massage treatment. Helps release fascial restrictions to eliminate pain and restore movement. Trained in treating headache, migraine, neck pain, back pain, pelvic pain, fibromyalgia and many other conditions.

Aromatherapy

NHS accredited aromatherapy treatment, trained at the Natural Health School — the only NHS-based complementary therapy school. Approved to provide treatments to NHS referred patients.

Private Sessions

Personalised one-to-one sessions tailored to your needs. Available at clinic locations across the Harrogate area, or at your home by arrangement.
